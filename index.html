Limited Licensed Psychologist

I am a Clinical Psychologist with a Masters Degree in School and Community Psychology from Wayne State University. My undergraduate degrees include Christian Theology and Psychology. I have been working in this field for almost thirty years. My background includes a lot of teaching in universities and colleges, facilitating many workshops and doing therapy for individuals, marital couples and families.

My approach in therapy is primarily cognitive restructuring; helping you restructure in your mind ways to work through the pain and the emotional struggles that life brings to you. I've also had extensive training in marital therapy.
